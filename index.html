I am currently an assistant professor at the School of Information Management and Engineering, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SHUFE). I received my PhD degree in computer science from The University of Hong Kong (HKU) in 2016, supervised by Prof. David Cheung and Prof. Nikos Mamoulis. Before that, I received my M. S. degree from the Department of Computer Science, East China Normal University (ECNU) in 2012, supervised by Prof. Shiliang Sun.
